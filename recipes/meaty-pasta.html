Meaty Pasta

My go-to "one pot" pasta recipe

Whenever I don't have any other ideas for dinner and I want to have something that still gives me the "home cooked meal" feeling I whip this up. It's pretty simple and uses ingredients I pretty much have all the time. Since getting a nice dutch oven I've been putting it under the broiler to finish off but even if that's not an option this still makes a nice homemade tasting, cheesy, filling pasta without having to use two different pots/pans.

Ideally this feeds 4 and takes 30-45 minutes but I'm usually cooking for just me and my girlfriend and I don't time myself so I'm sure I take longer.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow or sweet onion diced
  • 8oz of baby bella mushrooms cut into 1/4" slices
  • 1 pound ground beef (I like 80/20 but you do you)
  • 2-3 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 can of peeled, whole, San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1-2 tablespoon italian seasoning (I blend a teaspoon each dried oregano, basil, thyme, sometimes rosemary or parsley, whatever I'm feeling really)
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes (I just eyeball this)
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper (to taste at each stage I mention adding it)
  • 1 box rotini pasta (or whichever shape you'd like)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup? (I don't measure this) shredded mozzarella
  • however much grated parmesan your heart desires
